About Us

Our Story

Boneshakers Tattoo Studio has been serving the community with exceptional tattoo artistry and professional piercing services. Our team of dedicated artists brings years of combined experience and a passion for creating unique, meaningful body art.

We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and using only premium quality inks and equipment. Every client receives personalized attention, whether it's your first tattoo or you're adding to your collection.

Our studio provides a welcoming, comfortable environment where creativity thrives. We believe that getting a tattoo should be an enjoyable experience from consultation to completion.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about our services

How do I book an appointment?

+

You can book an appointment by calling us, sending a message through Facebook, or filling out the enquiry form on this page. We'll get back to you to discuss your ideas and schedule a consultation.

Do I need a deposit?

+

Yes, we require a deposit to secure your booking. This is non-refundable but will be deducted from your final tattoo price. Deposit amounts vary depending on the size of the project.

How should I prepare for my tattoo?

+

Get a good night's sleep, eat a proper meal beforehand, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours before your appointment. Wear comfortable, loose clothing that allows easy access to the area being tattooed.

What aftercare do you provide?

+

We provide detailed aftercare instructions and recommend quality aftercare products. Your artist will walk you through everything you need to know to ensure your tattoo heals perfectly.

Do you do cover-ups?

+

Yes! Several of our artists specialize in cover-up work. We also offer laser removal services if you need to lighten an existing tattoo before covering it. Book a consultation to discuss your options.

What age do you need to be?

+

You must be 18 or over to get a tattoo. We require valid photo ID. For piercings, those under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian with matching ID.
